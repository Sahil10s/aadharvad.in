About Us

Aadharvad Charitable Trust

Aadharvad Charitable Trust, Kalyan is non-profit, non-political, secular charitable trust, Serving to society since 2005, We work with underprivileged children, Women & their families, regardless of their caste, creed or religion.

Our Vision is to enable every child & women empowerment to attain the right to well-being & happiness. Our projects include supporting children in the 5 to 17age group & the women working in red light area in Kalyan through our Trust & Sponsorship for their empowerment. Through our Trust & Sponsorship Programme. We are providing education, health & nutrition to underprivileged women & children and youth, to empower them to reach their full potential, become employable and be-responsible citizens. We also provide support to families and elderly members of the community, through income generation activities and health interventions, with focus on those diagnosed with major illness.

We also cater to children with special needs at our Centre. The Centre caters to an average of 100+ children every year. The primary objective of the Child Care Center is to nurture the children, providing them with a professionally managed loving home, full of care, warmth and an opportunity for physical, emotional and intellectual growth, until they are rehabilitated within suitable adoptive families through legal procedures , at the earliest. All of this requires stretching our resources (both human and financial) to ensure that each child receives care, nutritional support and medical intervention that she has a right to, with the help of caregivers, nurses and staff.
